Common Questions

How many laser sessions will I need?
While many clients see a massive "glow up" after just one session, we typically recommend a protocol of 3 to 6 sessions spaced a month apart for profound, structural collagen rebuilding and deep pigment removal.
Is there downtime for Bio-Cellular Sculpting?
There is minimal to zero downtime. You might experience slight swelling or bruising at the injection sites for 24-48 hours, but the vast majority of our patients return to their regular social activities immediately.
What makes Dr. Johan's technique different?
Dr. Johan focuses on "undecteable enhancements." Instead of freezing the face or overfilling, he uses advanced micro-dosing and targeted deep-plane structural support so you look refreshed, vibrant, and entirely natural.
