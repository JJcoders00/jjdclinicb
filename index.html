Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?
Yes, to ensure minimal waiting time and that Dr. Johan can dedicate appropriate time to your consultation, we operate strictly on a prior appointment basis.
What should I bring to my first consultation?
Please come with a clean face (minimal to no makeup) and bring a list or photos of your current skincare products and any prescription medications you are taking.
Are laser treatments painful?
Most modern lasers cause minimal discomfort, often described as a slight snapping sensation. For deeper treatments, we apply a premium topical numbing cream prior to the procedure to ensure you are completely comfortable.
